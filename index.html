The Tradegy

Description:

a story where all humanity got teleported into a whole new world called Isel, with no grass, no resources, just a big world with endless mountains and dirt.
in this new world, there is a strange phenomenom that randomly hits people, causing them to get teleported into a much bigger world than the current one.
this world is called the Astonia, there, the teleported humans will have to clear the areas that they're teleported to inorder to survive and return to the Isel.
bringing with them along the way resources from Astonia To help rebuild humanity on the Isel.
